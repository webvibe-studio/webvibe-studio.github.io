Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How long does it take to create a website?

Timing depends on the complexity of the project. Typically, a landing page takes from one day to one week, a corporate website from one to two weeks, and an online store from two to four weeks. We always try to accommodate your timelines and requests.

2. What is the pricing policy for website creation?

Prices vary depending on the type of website and its functionality. For example, a landing page can cost from €49, while an online store starts from €249. A detailed price list can be found on our website.

3. What is included in the cost of the service?

The cost includes design development, page layout, CMS setup (if necessary), basic SEO optimization, and testing the website before launch. Additional services, such as copywriting or promotion, can be included for an extra fee.

4. Do you provide website promotion services?

Yes, we offer SEO promotion, contextual advertising, and SMM services. We can help you attract your target audience and increase your website's visibility in search engines.

5. Can I edit the website myself after it’s created?

Yes, we create websites on user-friendly CMS platforms that allow you to edit content yourself. We also provide training on how to manage your website.

6. How does the project work process proceed?

The process includes several stages: discussing requirements, creating a mockup, designing, programming, testing, and launching the website. At each stage, you will receive updates and be able to make changes.

7. Do you provide technical support after the project is completed?

Yes, we offer various technical support packages, including bug fixes, content updates, and technical consultations.

8. What type of website should I choose?

This depends on your goals and budget. We can conduct a consultation to understand your needs and suggest the best solution.

9. What technologies do you use for website development?

We use modern technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, as well as popular CMS like WordPress and Joomla, and e-commerce systems like WooCommerce or OpenCart.

10. How is payment for services made?

We offer various payment options, including upfront payment and installment payments based on project progress. Details can be discussed during negotiations.
