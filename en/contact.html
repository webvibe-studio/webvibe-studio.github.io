Don't hesitate—call or write! Everyone can afford a website.

If you’ve decided to order a website, follow these simple steps:

  1. Define the purpose of the website: Think about what you need it for. It could be a business portal, online store, or blog—it's important to clearly understand your task.
  2. Choose a name and domain: Come up with a memorable name and select an appropriate domain zone (.com, .ua, etc.). Make sure the domain reflects the essence of your project.
  3. Gather necessary information: Prepare all materials, including contact details, photos, and descriptions of products or services. This will help make the website informative and attractive.
  4. Contact me: After you have gathered the information, write to me. We will discuss the details of your project and make an advance payment.
  5. Choose hosting: I will help you select a reliable hosting provider to ensure the stable operation of your website and its accessibility for users.
  6. Testing and training: After the work is completed, test the website to ensure its functionality. If needed, I will help train your employees.
  7. Website support: Discuss the terms of technical support so that your website remains relevant and operates smoothly.
